About RoseTech

Cultivating Innovation in the Heart of Central Asia

Kyrgyzstan Rose University of Science and Technology (RoseTech) is a premier private research university located in Bishkek. As a core member of the "New Silk Road" Education Alliance, we merge Smart Engineering with Sustainability.

Formerly the renowned "ROSE Institute," our university stands as a beacon of digital transformation. We don't just teach technology; we nurture the wisdom to use it responsibly, symbolizing a rose blooming in the arid landscape of challenges.
