Message from the President

Where Wisdom Blooms and the Future Begins

Dear Students, Colleagues, and Global Partners,

Welcome to Kyrgyzstan Rose University of Science and Technology (RoseTech). It is my distinct honor to welcome you to our vibrant community in the heart of Bishkek.

Many ask me about the origin of our name. In the rugged terrains of Central Asia, a rose is not merely a flower; it is a symbol of resilience, complexity, and the unfolding of life. At RoseTech, we believe education is an organic process. Like a rose, it requires strong roots in fundamental science, but its ultimate purpose is to bloom.

We are not just a university; we are a laboratory for the future. From our "Turing Library" to the advanced "Smart Engineering Labs," every brick on this campus is laid with the intention of fostering innovation.

I invite you to join us on this exciting journey.

Dr. Azamat S. Karimov, Ph.D.

President, Kyrgyzstan Rose University of Science and Technology

CS101: Introduction to Programming & Computational Thinking

School of Computer Science & AI • Fall 2025

Course Description

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to computer science and the art of programming. Using Python as the primary language and C++ for performance analysis, students will learn to think like computer scientists. The curriculum focuses on abstraction, algorithms, data structures, and software engineering principles.
