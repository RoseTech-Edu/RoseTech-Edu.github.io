About RoseTech

Bridging Tradition and Technology in Bishkek

Kyrgyzstan Rose University of Science and Technology (RoseTech) is a premier private research university. As a core member of the "New Silk Road" Education Alliance, we seamlessly integrate Smart Engineering with Sustainability.

Formerly the renowned "ROSE Institute of Smart Engineering," our institution has evolved into a beacon of digital transformation. We don't just teach technology; we cultivate the wisdom to apply it responsibly, symbolizing a rose blooming in a landscape of new challenges.
